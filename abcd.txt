Role Responsibilities and Outcomes

ADMIN
- Manage users: create, update, delete, and assign roles
- Manage insurance policies: create new policies, update policy details, and track expiration
- Manage hospitals and doctors: maintain provider directories for reporting and analytics
- Process payments: settle approved reimbursement claims and mark payments completed
- View claims: access all claims, including pending, approved, rejected, and settled
- Access analytics dashboard: review claim trends, hospital and doctor performance, and high-risk users

APPROVER
- View pending claims: access reimbursement claims waiting for review
- Verify treatment details: confirm diagnosis, treatment description, amount, and date
- Verify hospital information: review hospital name, address, and contact details
- Verify doctor information: review doctor name, specialization, qualification, and experience
- Verify uploaded documents: validate medical reports, prescriptions, bills, and receipts
- Review recommendation score: use advisory recommendation status and reasons as guidance
- Approve claims: authorize approved reimbursement claims for payment processing
- Reject claims with comments: provide rejection reasons and feedback for denied claims

POLICYHOLDER
- Submit reimbursement claims: raise claims after paying for treatment outside the system
- Enter treatment information: provide diagnosis, treatment details, provider name, and expense data
- Upload supporting documents: submit medical reports, prescriptions, bills, and receipts
- Track claim status: follow the lifecycle of the claim through pending, approved, rejected, and settled
- View claim decisions: review approver feedback, approval amounts, or rejection reasons

Outcomes by Role
- ADMIN makes system configuration, policy maintenance, user and provider administration, payment settlement, and analytics review.
- APPROVER makes claim decisions, validates claim evidence, and applies final approval or rejection.
- POLICYHOLDER makes reimbursement submissions, supplies treatment evidence, and monitors claim progress.
